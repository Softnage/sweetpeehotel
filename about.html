Our Story

Founded in 2010, Sweet Pee Hotel has been a beacon of luxury and hospitality on the stunning coastline. What started as a boutique beachfront retreat has evolved into one of the most prestigious hotels in the region, while maintaining its commitment to personalized service and authentic experiences.

  • Over 15 years of excellence
  • Family-owned and operated
  • Award-winning service
  • Continuous innovation

Our Mission

We strive to create unforgettable experiences for our guests by combining luxury accommodation with exceptional service and sustainable practices. Our commitment to excellence extends beyond hospitality to environmental stewardship and community engagement.

  • Exceptional guest experience

ROOMS

We offer three types of rooms to cater to your needs:

  • Deluxe Room: Spacious and elegantly designed, featuring a king-sized bed, ocean view, and modern Facilities.
  • Executive Suite: Luxurious suite with a separate living area, premium furnishings, and exclusive access to the executive lounge.
  • Family Suite: Perfect for families, offering multiple bedrooms, a kitchenette, and a private balcony with stunning views.
